discovered the passage a passion for typography, writing and design.

[Smashing Magazine](https://www.smashingmagazine.com),  a leading online magazine 
dedicated to design and web development. Vitaly is the author, 
co-author and editor of all [Smashing books](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/printed-books/)
He currently works developing the new [Smashing Memberships](https://www.smashingmagazine.com/membership) around the globe!
